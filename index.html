💡 Tips

Reduce Your Footprint

Small changes can make a big difference. Here are science-backed ways to lower your emissions.

🚲

Choose Active Transport

Walking, cycling, or using public transport for short trips can cut transport emissions by up to 50%.

💡

Switch to LEDs

LED bulbs use 75% less energy than incandescent lighting and last 25 times longer.

🥦

Eat More Plants

A plant-rich diet can reduce food-related emissions by 50–70% compared to a heavy meat diet.

♻️

Recycle & Reuse

Recycling aluminum saves 95% of the energy needed to make new aluminum from raw materials.

🌡️

Optimize Thermostat

Adjusting your thermostat by just 1°C can reduce heating bills and emissions by up to 10%.

🛒

Buy Local & Seasonal

Locally sourced food travels fewer miles, reducing transport-related emissions significantly.
